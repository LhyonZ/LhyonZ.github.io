Life project

It is said that the only constant in our lives is change, that everything changes over time and that if there are no more changes in our lives we should start to worry, so we must embrace change wherever it comes from, whether it’s the end of an educational stage employment, social, etc,,,, or elsewhere within ourselves.

Maybe when one of those moments of making changes arrives, we want to jump into the adventure to see what happens and improvise, and well that is ok, but not everyone can just go and that’s it, There are those who have responsibilities and need to plan a little, make what are called “life plans” or in practice, make personal budgets.

Personal budgets are lists of expenses that a person must consider in order to maintain a desired standard of living; In these lists, everyone considers different things, because we are not all the same and we give different importance to things and even if we are in a hurry to start a new stage in our life, there are those who could even sacrifice certain expenses.

the app

LifeProject is a free application developed for mobile devices (cell phones and tablets) with Android Operating System, in which you can create and track your life projects from a budgetary point of view, you can create lists of things you think will spend your money and how much you are willing to invest in them. You can even create and compare different cases of the same project to understand the difference in expenses between them; In the end, you’ll know how much you need per day, week or month and what salary or income you need to make your idea a reality
