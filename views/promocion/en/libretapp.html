Android App LibretApp

LibretApp is an application designed for entrepreneurs who are just starting out in the world of sales. Often, it is better to start a business by making sales on credit; this encourages your customers to try your services today and pay later.

With this application you will be able to register your clients, the different sales you make with them, and the payments made on those sales, so you will know the date of their payments and how much they still owe you.
