how to create a strong password

Creating a password is as simple as thinking of a word or phrase that will protect the access to something important to us, such as a social media profile or a bank account, and above all, trying not to forget it, well, nowadays it can’t be just any word or phrase, it shouldn’t be things like 12345678 or abcdefgh because they have been widely used, or things that are easy to guess, like personal data or information about people close to you.

Also depending on what we want to protect, we should take certain considerations, such as defining the length of the password, protecting our conversations is not the same as protecting important documents, that’s why there are different levels of security in passwords. Today, password lengths of 8 to 11 characters are considered weak, while 12 to 15 are considered good and 16 or more are considered strong passwords, it is also good practice to change important passwords every 3 months and not use the same password for more than one service.

Another recommendation is not to use only letters from ‘a’ to ‘z’, but to add more security you can add special characters (symbols !, @, #, etc..), numbers or blank spaces and consider mixing uppercase letters with lowercase letters.

about the app
