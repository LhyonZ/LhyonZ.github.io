Android App LifeProject

With this application, you will be able to create life projects such as a trip, your foray into a new hobby, the study of a course or school grade, or moving to a different city.

In the application, you will be able to record the expenses you consider for each project such as transportation, services, rent, or food, create different scenarios like the case in which you spend less or waste money, and finally calculate how much income you need to carry out those projects.
