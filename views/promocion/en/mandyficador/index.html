Mandificador

grocery list app

the app

Mandyficador is a free application developed for mobile devices (cell phones and tablets) with Android operating system, in which you can create shopping lists and track them (mark which products you have found). Also with this application you can keep track of your pantry, this will allow you to create smart lists (with the products that you don’t have in house).

shopping lists

When we go out looking for products, we can’t always find them in a single store, or sometimes we know they’re cheaper elsewhere. Witch Mandyficador, you can create as many lists as you want: list by product type, by business type or by the business name itself, and work on them separately.

pantry lists

To know the status of your pantry, you can record which products and how much of them you want at home. You can also record which ones you’re using, which ones you’ve managed to stock up on during an outing, and request smart lists with the missing products.
