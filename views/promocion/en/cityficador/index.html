Cityficador

appointment control app

the app

Cityficador is a free app developed for mobiles devices (cell phones and tablets) with the Android Operating System. It allows you to keep track of your work meetings with clientes, whether you offer beauty, medical specialized consulting or technical services (installation or maintenance), as well as your appointments with providers of services or products, family meetings, social events, or from the church.

scheduling clients

In the application you can register your contacts, whether they are consumers or suppliers, and keep track of meetings with them, You will be able to know who you should visit on a certain day, and receive notifications so you can be aware of your events.

The tool is designed for people who either due to their work or personal needs, have an active social life and want to stay aware of their commitments; people with jobs, professionals, entrepreneurs, suppliers, employees with more responsibility, or citizens who consume these businesses.
