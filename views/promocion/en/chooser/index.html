Chooser

decision-making app

desicion-making

Even if we don’t realize it or it doesn’t seem like it, in out daily lives we make hundreds of decisions, because eating one more bite or continuing walking iS already a decision, either continuing to do the same things you’ve been up to this point or taking a new direction in your life are also choices.

As you can see, some of the decisions we face, and in fact most of them, are simple things that we sometimes solve by following goals that we had already pre-established in the past, such as being fitter or choosing the path of good, things like get a soda or a bottled water at the store, although there are also other decisions and perhaps one of them brings you here, more relevant decisions with a greater impact on out lives, which make us think a little more, things like choosing a career or our next address.

This is why Chooser was created, a free application for mobile devices (cell phones and tablets) with Android Operating System, with tools to accompany you in different types of decision-making processes.

about the app

Chooser has a feature that lets you record your everyday situations-simple decisions like choosing today’s dinner or what series to watch this afternoon and lists your favorite options, so whenever you need a quick recommendation, you can consult the app with the help of chance.

For more important matters, the tool offer you a more comprehensive approach, guiding you through a process that will help you to determine wich option is most relevant to you, or what best suits you based on your current interests. This way, you’ll have a clear idea of your preferences before consulting with your trusted people, or God.
