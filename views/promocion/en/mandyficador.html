Android App Mandificador

With this app you can create shopping lists (per store or per shopping trip) register which product you must get in each occasion and while you are in the shoppings, you can check which products you find. Also with this app you can register a kitchen cupboard with the products you wish to have on it, register which products you have or find in your lists and create smart lists with the products you need to find.
