how to save money

Savings are an important part of our lives, as they provide us with a financial cushion that allows us to respond in the event of an unforeseen event, such as carrying out repairs or lack or work, also they give us a sense of security and independence with which we can sometimes even opt for better opportunities by helping us cope with times of transition more calmly.

There are two type of people in the world, the first, who are not characterized by thinking much about the future, which is not bad either, as they prefer to live in the moment, in special cases, they usually use other tools such as credits to respond; for the second type of people, these actions could be a little stressful, they save a portion of their income, sometimes what is left over after expenses and other times an amount that they pre-established at some point in their life.

How much of their income should a person save to have a healthy economy?, some persons might feel like they don’t save enough, while other persons might save more and miss out on opportunities or even get frustrated because they feel like they work and don’t enjoy the fruits of their labor, someone once said that we should learn to live on 70% of our salary and allocate the rest as follows: 10% for spiritual causes (or pay debs), 10% to save and the rest to invest. In this way, and this is the answer.

And what we can do with these savings? Well, we can count on them for unforeseen events, or combine them with the portion intended for investments and think about investment projects like buying tools or things that allow us to save either in expenses or time such as buying a car or things that increase in value over time, like land. If you still have some of your other 70% left, consider saving it up to get a better cellphone, plan a trip, or whatever.

about the app

Ahorrificador is a free application developed for mobiles devices (cell phones and tablets) with Android operating system, in which you can keep track of how much you have saved (in a piggy bank, a bank account or under the mattress) and create saving projects where you can allocate those savings, this way you can save for several things at the same time and allocate different amounts to each account, managing to stay more involved with you savings.
